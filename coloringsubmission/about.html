About the project

Coloring pages powered by community photos.

ColoringSubmission.com turns submitted photos into joyful coloring book pages, then uses those creations to build books for families and donate kid-friendly collections to hospitals, schools, daycares, waiting rooms, and other community spaces where creativity can brighten the day.

Our mission

We believe coloring is more than a quiet activity. It helps children relax, express themselves, practice fine motor skills, and feel connected to the world around them. By transforming real submitted photos into coloring pages, we create books that feel personal, familiar, and full of life.

Every submission helps us grow a library of printable artwork that can be shared through donated coloring books for hospitals, schools, daycares, and community programs. Our goal is simple: collect meaningful images, turn them into accessible art, and put more creative moments into kids' hands.

How it works for submitters

1

Submit a clear photo

Send us a family-safe image with a strong subject, such as a pet, animal, nature scene, toy, vehicle, landmark, or other creative idea. Bright, uncluttered photos usually become the best coloring pages.

2

We convert it into line art

Our team reviews submissions and turns selected photos into black-and-white coloring pages with clean outlines. We may simplify backgrounds or adjust details so the final page is fun and easy to color.

3

Pages become books and donations

Finished pages may be included in themed coloring books, digital downloads, printed products, or donation batches sent to hospitals, schools, daycares, and local organizations.

Photo rights

By submitting, you grant us permission to convert your photo into coloring book pages. You retain ownership of the original photo.

You also confirm that you have the right to share the photo with us and that it does not violate another person's copyright, privacy, or publicity rights. We may decline or remove submissions that are unsafe, inappropriate, unclear, or unsuitable for children's coloring content.

Privacy policy

  • We collect information you provide, such as your name, email address, submitted photo, and notes about your submission.
  • We use that information to review submissions, create coloring pages, contact you about personalization or delivery, and improve the project.
  • We do not sell personal contact information. We may share limited information with service providers only when needed for hosting, email, payment, printing, shipping, or customer support.
  • If a submitted photo contains a child, identifying details, addresses, school names, license plates, or private information, we may crop, simplify, reject, or request a replacement image.
  • You may contact us to request correction or removal of personal information where reasonably possible.

Terms of service

  • Submissions must be lawful, family-friendly, and owned by you or shared with permission from the rights holder.
  • We may edit, crop, trace, stylize, combine, publish, sell, donate, or decline submitted material as part of creating coloring pages and books.
  • Digital files are for personal, classroom, daycare, hospital, or organizational use unless a separate commercial license is provided.
  • Physical print timelines are estimates and may vary due to customization, printing, shipping, holidays, or supplier delays.
  • We make reasonable efforts to produce high-quality pages, but final artwork may differ from the original photo because it is simplified for coloring.

Donations and community use

Donation availability depends on submission volume, production costs, and partner needs. We prioritize kid-safe, broadly appealing pages that work well for group settings and can be enjoyed by children with different coloring skill levels.

If your school, daycare, hospital, clinic, library, or nonprofit would like to request donated coloring pages or books, please reach out and tell us about your group.
