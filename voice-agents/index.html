Frequently asked questions

Does this use real phone numbers?

Yes. Your business gets a dedicated phone number. The AI answers calls, texts missed callers, and routes urgent calls to you.

What if the AI can't answer a question?

The AI transfers the call or sends you a detailed message. You set the rules — no AI handles anything outside your comfort zone.

Do customers know they're talking to AI?

Yes — the AI introduces itself as an AI assistant. Transparency builds trust and sets expectations.

How fast is the text-back?

Typically 2-5 seconds after a missed call. Fast enough that most customers haven't even left your voicemail yet.
