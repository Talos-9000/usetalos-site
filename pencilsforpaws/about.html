Our story

Art for kids, keepsakes for pet families.

Pencils for Paws was created for families who know pets are part of the family story. We turn everyday animal photos into coloring pages that kids can personalize, share, and save.

Story

Bailey bought pencilsforpaws.com as a companion home for pet-first coloring books: warmer and more focused than a general coloring submission site, with room for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, fish, farm animals, and one-of-a-kind pets.

Mission

Our mission is to make creative family time more personal while celebrating animals with kindness. We design pages that are friendly for children, useful for classrooms, and meaningful for pet lovers.

Photo Rights Policy

You should only submit photos you own or have permission to use. By submitting, you allow Pencils for Paws to create derivative coloring-page art, email it to you, display approved art in our gallery, and include it in family-friendly digital or print products. We use pet first names only and avoid publishing private personal details.

Privacy

We collect submission details needed to review photos, create artwork, and deliver files by email. We do not ask children to submit directly. Parents or guardians should handle submissions. We do not sell email lists or publish addresses, phone numbers, or full family names.

Terms

Pencils for Paws products are provided for personal, classroom, and family use unless a product license says otherwise. Do not resell, repackage, or redistribute purchased PDFs. Submission does not guarantee publication, and we may reject photos that are blurry, unsafe, copyrighted by someone else, or not family-friendly.

Family Promise

We keep the tone gentle, the designs printable, and the experience clear. Our goal is joyful coloring pages that make kids smile and make pet owners say, “That looks just like them.”
